Our products

Disposable Biopsy Forcep
Medivice biopsy forceps feature excellent construction and provide reliable performance. They are available in sizes that will fit down to a 2.4mm scope channel and are designed for easy positioning to the target biopsy site.
Disposable Polypectomy Snare
Medivice polypectomy snare is designed for easy insertion into GI endoscopic channels and features soft, flexible wires that require less force to open and close loops. This is a hybrid product that allows the user to choose a cold or hot configuration as needed during the procedure.
Medivice Niddle
The Medivice Injection Needle provides a user-friendly and reliable injection performance. Improved processing techniques enable sharper needle tips than previous needles, providing better puncture and injectability.
Package
Explore our Essential Endoscopic Set, tailored for efficiency and precision in medical procedures. This package includes three key tools: a biopsy forcep, a polypectomy snare, and an injection needle. Each item is designed for optimal performance and ease of use, making them ideal for a variety of endoscopic applications.
